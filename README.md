# Sales & Customer Dashboard | Tableau Project

## Overview
Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze sales performance and customer behavior using business-oriented KPIs and visual storytelling. The project is designed to help stakeholders monitor business performance, identify trends, and derive actionable insights through a clean and interactive dashboard interface.

## Business Objective
The objective of this project is to transform raw sales and customer data into a reporting dashboard that supports performance monitoring, customer analysis, and decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies
- Tableau

## Dashboard Scope
This project consists of two business dashboards:

### 1. Sales Dashboard
Focused on analyzing business performance through:
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity
- Sales & Profit by Subcategory
- Sales & Profit Trends Over Time
- Year-over-Year performance comparison

### 2. Customer Dashboard
Focused on customer-related business insights through:
- Total Customers
- Total Sales per Customer
- Total Orders
- Customer Distribution by Number of Orders
- Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Customer performance tracking

## Key Business Insights
- Tracked overall sales, profit, and quantity performance using KPI cards
- Compared current year performance against previous year trends
- Identified top-performing and underperforming product subcategories
- Analyzed weekly sales and profit movement over time
- Evaluated customer purchasing behavior and order distribution
- Identified top customers contributing to profit and revenue

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive and business-friendly dashboard design
- KPI-driven performance reporting
- Trend analysis with year-over-year comparison
- Customer segmentation and ranking insights
- Clean visual storytelling for easier interpretation
- Filter-based dashboard navigation

## Dataset
The dataset used for this project contains sales, profit, quantity, customer, and order-related business information required for performance analysis and dashboard reporting.
